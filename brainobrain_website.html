Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our program

What is the right age to start abacus training? +

The ideal age to start abacus training is between 4-14 years. At this age, children's brains are most receptive to new learning patterns and can easily adapt to the abacus methodology. We have age-specific programs designed for different developmental stages.

How long does it take to see results? +

Most parents notice improvements in their child's concentration and math confidence within 2-3 months. Significant academic improvements typically occur within 6 months. Our structured 10-level program ensures steady progress throughout the journey.

What makes BrainoBrain different from other programs? +

Our program combines traditional abacus training with modern teaching methods. We focus on whole brain development, not just calculation skills. With certified instructors, small class sizes, and a proven international curriculum, we ensure quality education for every child.

Is the free trial class really free? +

Yes, absolutely! Our trial class is completely free with no hidden charges or obligations. It's our way of letting you and your child experience our teaching methodology before making any commitment.

What if my child doesn't like math? +

That's exactly why our program works! We make math fun and engaging through games, activities, and hands-on learning. Many children who initially disliked math have become math enthusiasts after joining our program. The confidence they gain transforms their entire attitude toward learning.
